Help

We are a website where each user can upload products they no longer need, without expecting anything in return, just like a library of objects.

Our users are little soldiers fighting in the battle against climate change, applying what we call the three Rs: "Recycle, Reuse, and Reduce." Through our application, we aim to reduce production and, consequently, the waste generated from it. Additionally, we solve the problem of unused items by giving them a purpose and preventing them from collecting dust in other people's homes.

Every user who signs up for the application commits to taking the utmost care of all the objects, treating them as if they were their own.

Thank you, The Cosateca.
